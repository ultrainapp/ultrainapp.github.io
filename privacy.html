The short version: ulTrain does not sell your data, does not run ads, and does not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your data is saved to your account database by default and is never visible to or shared with other users — unless you explicitly choose to share it.

1. Who We Are

ulTrain is an iOS application developed and operated as an independent app. References to "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to the developer of ulTrain.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the ulTrain app ("App") on your iOS device. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the App's functionality. When you create an account, your data is saved to a secure database tied exclusively to your user account. No other user can see or access your data.

Account Information

Your email address and a password hash, used solely for authentication and account recovery. This is the key that locks your data to you.

Training Data

Runs, training plans, weekly targets, and progress you log in the App — including distances, pace, effort ratings, and notes. Saved to your account database by default.

Nutrition & Preferences

Nutrition logs, food ratings, what-works notes, and app preferences (units, notification settings, etc.). All stored in your account and never shared.

Health & Activity Data

With your explicit permission, the App reads activity and workout data from Apple Health. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

We do not collect:

  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Any data not directly related to your training

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve the App's core features:

  • Displaying your training plans, runs, progress charts, and statistics
  • Enabling the AI Coach to generate personalised training insights on-device
  • Sending push notifications about plan reminders and activity imports (only if you grant permission)
  • Restoring your data from cloud backup when you reinstall or switch devices (only if cloud sync is enabled)
  • Processing in-app subscription purchases through Apple's StoreKit

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the App.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is never visible to other users of the App unless you explicitly choose to share it.

The only content shared between users is:

  • Events (races): If you add a race to the directory and choose to make it public, that race listing becomes visible to all users. This is opt-in — you can keep races private, and you can change this at any time.
  • Event reviews: Reviews you write for races in the directory are shared publicly within the App. Do not include personal information in reviews you wish to keep private.

Everything else — your runs, training plans, nutrition logs, kit, preferences, and personal details — is strictly private to your account and never shared with other users.

The only cases where data is processed by a third party infrastructure provider are:

  • Firebase (Google): Your account data is stored in Firebase Firestore and protected by Firebase Authentication. Firebase processes this data solely to store and return it to you. Google's data processing terms apply.
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We receive only a transaction receipt confirming your subscription status — no payment card details.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

There are no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or behavioural tracking frameworks in the App.

5. Health & Fitness Data

ulTrain can read workout and activity data from Apple Health to automatically import your completed activities into your training plan. This is entirely opt-in and requires you to grant explicit permission through the standard iOS Health permission dialog.

  • Health data is read directly from HealthKit on your device
  • It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • It is stored locally on your device within the App's data store
  • You can revoke Health access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

We comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines, which prohibit using health data for advertising or selling it to data brokers.

6. Database Storage & iCloud Backup

When you create an account, your data — including runs, plans, nutrition logs, kit, and preferences — is saved to a secure database (Firebase Firestore) tied to your account. This is the default behaviour and is what allows you to access your data when you reinstall the App or switch devices.

Your data in the database is:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS
  • Accessible only by you via your authenticated account
  • Never visible to or shared with other users (except for race listings and reviews you explicitly make public)
  • Permanently deleted when you delete your account

iCloud Backup is a separate, optional feature. Enabling it creates a backup of your data in Apple's iCloud, giving you an additional restore point. iCloud backups:

  • Are entirely opt-in and can be disabled at any time from within the App's settings menu
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the App's settings menu
  • Are governed by Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy in addition to this policy

7. AI Coach & On-Device Processing

The AI Coach feature generates personalised training insights and recommendations. This feature is powered by on-device machine learning using Apple's on-device AI framework — no training data, prompts, or responses are sent to any external server.

  • All AI inference runs locally on your iPhone
  • Your training context is never transmitted to us or Apple's cloud
  • The feature requires a device that supports Apple Intelligence

This means the AI Coach works completely privately, with no data leaving your device at any point.

8. Subscriptions & Payments

ulTrain offers optional premium subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. All billing is handled by Apple — we never receive, process, or store your payment card details.

We receive only a transaction receipt from Apple confirming your subscription status. This is used solely to unlock premium features within the App.

For billing questions, cancellations, or refunds, please manage your subscription through the App Store or contact Apple Support.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored in our database for as long as your account exists. When your account is deleted, all data associated with your user account is permanently removed — including runs, training plans, nutrition logs, kit, preferences, race listings you added, and any reviews you wrote.

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account within the App
  • This immediately deletes your Firebase Authentication account and all Firestore data associated with your user ID
  • Any iCloud backup data can also be removed from within the App's settings menu before or after account deletion
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

If you delete the App without first deleting your account, your database data is retained until you sign back in and delete your account, or until you contact us to request deletion.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, correct, export, or delete it. We support all of these:

  • Access & Export: Your training data is visible within the App at all times. You can export it from Settings → Backup.
  • Correction: You can edit any data directly within the App.
  • Deletion: Use the Delete Account option in Settings, or email us and we will delete your data within 30 days.
  • Portability: Your backup export is in a standard JSON format you can use with other tools.

To exercise any right or make a privacy request, contact us using the details below.

11. Children's Privacy

ulTrain is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the App.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your data rights, or need to report a privacy concern, please get in touch.

Privacy Questions & Requests

We take privacy seriously and aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.
