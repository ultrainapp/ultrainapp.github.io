Training Plans

Volume-first plans that fit around your real life and race calendar.

  • Create plans with race name, date, distance, activity type (run / walk / hike) and optional elevation tracking.
  • Auto-generate weekly targets from configurable build, rest, taper, and race multipliers.
  • Edit, complete (with finish time), cancel (with reason), or delete plans at any time.
  • Sort by race date, name, progress, or creation date and filter by activity type.
  • Show or hide archived (completed / cancelled) plans to keep your list tidy.

Training Weeks

Every plan broken into weeks so you always know the work ahead.

  • Auto-generated weekly breakdown with phase assignment (Build, Rest, Taper, Race, Injury).
  • Edit individual week distance and elevation targets when life happens.
  • Mark weeks as injured to automatically zero out targets and protect recovery.
  • Current week highlighting so you always know exactly where you are in the block.

Run Logging

One log for everything you do, across all plans.

  • Log runs with date, type, distance, duration, elevation, and notes.
  • Use built-in activity types or create custom ones with icons.
  • Edit and delete logged runs whenever you need to fix the record.
  • Manage all runs across all plans from a single Settings view.

Apple Health

Let your watch do the recording, ulTrain keeps it organised.

  • Import workouts (run, walk, hike) from Apple Health with distance, duration, elevation, heart rate, cadence, and active calories.
  • Manually browse and select activities with filters by type.
  • Swipe to ignore specific workouts you don’t want to log.

Progress & Charts

See the block from 10,000ft or zoom into each week.

  • Overall plan progress showing percentage complete and distance vs target.
  • Per-week progress bars with optional elevation tracking.
  • Days-to-race countdown so you always know how much time is left.
  • Pro: weekly distance, elevation, and time charts plus cumulative distance and phase breakdown.

Nutrition & Kit (Pro)

Dial in what you carry and what you take in.

  • Log nutrition items per activity with quantity, notes, and a 1–5 feeling rating.
  • See a “What Works” view ranked by average feeling, with category filters and search.
  • Manage a nutrition catalogue of items and categories with icons.
  • Create kit lists (standalone or plan-attached) with mandatory / optional items, notes, and packing checklist.
  • Save reusable kit templates for different race types.
  • AI-powered nutrition and kit insight to spot gaps and patterns.

Race Directory & AI Coach (Pro)

Find races, attach plans, and get smart guidance.

  • Browse and search a public race directory with filters for activity, distance, season, and country / region.
  • Link races directly to training plans or create custom races.
  • Opt-in public sharing and community reviews for real-world feedback.
  • AI-generated training plan summaries, nutrition analysis, and kit readiness checks.

Profile, Settings & Sync

Make ulTrain feel like yours, and keep everything safe.

  • Profile with name, DOB, runner level, training days per week, preferred units, and avatar.
  • Customise themes, default plan parameters, activity types, nutrition categories, kit templates, and motivational quotes.
  • Configure home sort and filter defaults so your most important plan is always on top.
  • Cloud sync, offline support, and iCloud backup with manual or scheduled options and restore (including during onboarding).

Widgets (Pro, iOS 17+)

Keep your next big effort on your Home Screen.

  • Plan Progress widget in a small size.
  • Weekly Progress widgets so you see volume at a glance.
  • Days Until Race widgets that keep your next start line front of mind.
