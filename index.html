Your Data. Your Rules.

Private by default.
Always.

We don't harvest your data, sell it, or share it. ulTrain is built on a simple principle: your training data belongs to you and nobody else.

ulTrain was created to address a clear gap in endurance training technology. We have no advertising model, no data partnerships, and no commercial interest in what you train, eat, or race. Your data exists solely to serve your performance.

Nothing shared by default

Your runs, plans, nutrition logs, and kit lists are never shared. Ever.

On-device AI only

The AI Coach runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple Intelligence. Your training data is never sent to any server — not ours, not Apple's cloud, nobody's.

Sync is opt-in

Cloud backup is completely optional. You choose if and when to enable it. Turn it off anytime and your data stays local only.

No ads. No tracking.

ulTrain has no advertising, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no behavioural tracking. We make money from subscriptions — not from you.
