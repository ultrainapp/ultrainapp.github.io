Plan Setup Wizard

Your plan,
set up in minutes.

No spreadsheets. No guesswork. ulTrain's guided wizard walks you through every detail with simple pickers and sliders — not a wall of text fields.

1

Activity & race

Choose your activity type, name your plan, and set your target race and distance — or keep it open.

2

Dates & schedule

Pick your start date, preferred week start day, and race day. ulTrain counts the weeks automatically.

3

Starting volume

Drag a slider to set your Week 1 target distance. No typing required — just set it where you are today.

4

Build factors

Plain-English sliders explain exactly what each factor means: "+15% each build week", "rest at 70% of peak". No jargon.

5

Phase schedule

Preview the full week-by-week breakdown before committing. Edit any phase with a tap.

6

Confirm & create

Review a full summary — race day, total weeks, Week 1 target, phase split — then create your plan in one tap.

Your Data. Your Rules.

Private by default.
Always.

We don't harvest your data, sell it, or share it. ulTrain is built on a simple principle: your training data belongs to you and nobody else.

ulTrain was created to address a clear gap in endurance training technology. We have no advertising model, no data partnerships, and no commercial interest in what you train, eat, or race. Your data exists solely to serve your performance.

Nothing shared by default

Your runs, plans, nutrition logs, and kit lists are never shared. Ever.

On-device only

Your Personal Coach runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple Intelligence. Your training data is never sent to any server — not ours, not Apple's cloud, nobody's.

Sync is opt-in

Cloud backup is completely optional. You choose if and when to enable it. Turn it off anytime and your data stays local only.

No ads. No tracking.

ulTrain has no advertising, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no behavioural tracking. We make money from subscriptions — not from you.
